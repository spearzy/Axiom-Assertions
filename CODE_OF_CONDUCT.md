# Code of Conduct

Axiom aims to be a respectful, technically serious, and welcoming project.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions.
- Focus on the change, the evidence, and the reasoning.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Keep criticism concrete and professional.
- Help keep the repository useful for contributors with different experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Demeaning language, intimidation, or sustained hostility.
- Deliberate disruption, bad-faith engagement, or trolling.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that makes participation feel unsafe or unwelcome.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this code of conduct and may limit participation where needed to protect the project and its contributors.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, report it through GitHub or through the maintainer contact options available on GitHub. Reports will be reviewed on a best-effort basis.
